How it Works?
  1. Teacher or Student login through there particular logins
  2. Teacher can post new assignment and Students can answer them Before due date
  3. Teacher and student both have there dashboard to view certain functionalities
  4. The teacher can only give marks to there students and not other students
  5. Students can't give marks to there peers or update or delete the assignments
  6. There can only be one answer submission per student
  7. Students won't be allowed to update and delete answers after the assignment expires
  8. Teachers won't be allowed to update the assignment after the due date but they can delete it
  9. Teachers can give marks to a particular students answer
